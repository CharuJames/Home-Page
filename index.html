Welcome to My Homepage

My name is Charu Karakkaparambil James, and I am deeply passionate about using artificial intelligence to address complex and impactful challenges. My work primarily revolves around Natural Language Processing (NLP), where I specialize in exploring innovative techniques and methodologies to advance the field.

My research interests include Dynamic Topic Modeling, which seeks to understand and model how topics evolve over time, and Continual Learning, a paradigm that enables AI systems to learn and adapt continuously without forgetting previously acquired knowledge. Additionally, I focus on cross-lingual knowledge distillation, a cutting-edge approach to transferring knowledge between languages and enhancing the performance and generalization of AI models across diverse linguistic contexts.

Through my work, I strive to push the boundaries of AI to develop solutions that solve complex problems and bridge linguistic and cultural gaps, ultimately contributing to a more interconnected and informed world.
